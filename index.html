The London Squats Archive is an historical Wiki project to map evicted Squats in London. With your help we aim to create an open source visual tool to better understand the history of squatting culture in London.

To add a squat, please send the address and additional information to londonsquatsarchive@riseup.net

We have some information on these squats, but we still need their street numbers in order to add them to the map. If you know the history/former location of these projects, please email the information to: londonsquatsarchive@riseup.net
